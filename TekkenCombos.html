COMBO SAMPLER


WHAT KIND OF SORCERY IS THIS ?

This is a tool for you to have a nicer time working on your Tekken (or any other 4 button game really) combos. You fill in the text bar on top, click on the "Be pretty" button, and look at your prettified combo. If you want, you can then download it by clicking on the "Save as PNG" button.

BUT HOW DO I WRITE MY STUFF IN ?

This tool reads the inputs as they're commonly used in the tekken community, with a few limitations to ensure it understands what you write. See the table below for what associations are used.
You can also use a + sign between a direction and any button input (even one with a + sign in it !). It's merely a convenience thing, but it means both d/f 1 and d/f+1 for example are valid inputs for the same end result.
Finally, you need something to distinguish between your inputs. This tool uses either comma (",") or space (" ") as a separator. Either will work, so use whichever you're more confortable with.

ARE THERE ANY QUALITY OF LIFE THINGS I SHOULD KNOW ?

Why yes there are, actually.
You can use the semicolon (";") like you would use another input to get a visual separator (useful to distinguish between strings in a combo, for example).
You can also use ws (or WS) to get a neat little icon for While Standing moves.
Quarter circle forward and back are in (QCF / qcf and QCB / qcb respectively), as is Crouch Dash (CD / cd), so it's just this little bit easier to use.
As far as using your .pngs goes, I'd like to recommend 2 little pieces of software. First, some sort of Window-On-Top software, so you can get your combo to stay on top of the Tekken window when playing. I like DeskPins, easy to use, goes in your system notification area.
Then, you'll want a really minimalist image viewer so you don't lose too much space on your screen. I like pViewer for this. No menu, everything is done via mouse clicks, so you don't lose any more space than you need to.
